# Privacy Policy for Moments

**Effective Date:** December 26, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 26, 2025

## Overview

Moments ("we," "our," or "the app") is a personal video diary app that helps you capture and review daily moments. Your privacy is paramount. This policy explains how Moments handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Moments does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All videos, photos, and app preferences are stored **exclusively on your device**. We have no servers, no cloud storage, and no analytics.

## Permissions

Moments requests the following permissions to function:

- **Camera**: To record video clips
- **Microphone**: To capture audio with your video clips
- **Photos (Write-Only)**: To save your daily diary videos to your Photos library when you choose to export

**We only access these when you actively use the feature.** Data never leaves your device.

## Data Storage

- **Videos**: Stored locally in your device's file system
- **Preferences**: None stored in v1.0
- **No Cloud Upload**: Your content is never uploaded to our servers (we don't have any)

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.** There are no third-party services, advertisers, or analytics tools integrated into Moments.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

- **Individual Clips**: Select the clip and tap the delete button to delete it from the app
- **Generated Diaries**: Tap the delete button to delete it from the app 
- **All Data**: Delete the app from your device

## Children's Privacy

Moments does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since all data stays on-device, parental consent is not required.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Check this page periodically for updates.
